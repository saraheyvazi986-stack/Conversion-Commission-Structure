04 — Eligibility & Gate Rules

Qualifying Conditions

All conditions must be met for a deposit to count as a qualifying FTD and for the agent to be eligible for commission that month.

🏁
Minimum FTD Amount
The deposit must be ≥ $100 USD to qualify. Deposits below this threshold are recorded but do not count toward commission or the monthly FTD count for volume bonus purposes.
🔢
Minimum 10 FTDs / Month
An agent must bring in at least 10 qualifying FTDs in a calendar month to earn any commission for that month. Below this threshold, zero commission is paid — regardless of FTD size.
🧱
Hard Commission Cap
An agent's total monthly commission (per-FTD commissions + volume bonus, combined) is capped at $1,000 USD. Earnings beyond this threshold are not paid out and do not roll over to the following month.
🔗
Client Must Be Assigned to Agent
The depositing client must be registered under the agent's CRM account at the time of the FTD. Retroactive reassignments are not accepted. Disputed ownership defaults to first-touch attribution.
✅
KYC & Compliance Cleared
The client must be fully KYC-verified before commission is paid. FTDs made by unverified accounts are held in pending status and only credited once KYC is approved and no red flags exist.
⏱️
True First-Time Deposit
Commission is paid on the first deposit only. Repeat deposits, re-deposits after withdrawal, or duplicate accounts from the same client do not qualify. Duplicate detection runs on email, phone, and device fingerprint.
💳
Payment Source Validity
The deposit must be made from a payment method matching the client's verified identity. Third-party payments, crypto-to-fiat conversions under review, or chargebacks void the associated commission.
05 — Clawback & Adjustments

Commission Recovery Policy

Commission may be reversed or adjusted under the following conditions. These protect the company from abuse and incentivize agents to bring in quality clients, not just first deposits.

⚠️ Withdrawal Within 30 Days
If a client withdraws their full deposit within 30 calendar days of the FTD with zero trades placed, 100% of the commission is clawed back in the following month's payout.
🚫 Fraudulent / Related Parties
If a deposit is linked to fraud, money laundering, or a related party of the agent (family, friends used to inflate FTD count), the full commission plus a disciplinary deduction applies.
📅 Payout Window
Commissions for month M are reviewed during the first 5 business days of month M+1 and paid by the 10th of M+1, after clawback review. No advance payments.
⚡
The clawback window and withdrawal-without-trade rule are the most critical protections here. Without them, agents are incentivized to onboard anyone — including their own contacts depositing and withdrawing immediately. Consider tracking deposits with ≥1 trade placed as the gold standard quality metric over time.
